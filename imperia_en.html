Imperia Tower

  • Height: 238.6 m
  • Years of construction: 2006-2011
  • Developer: Solvers Estate
  • Total area: 203191 m2
  • Lift speed: 7 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 60 and 18
  • Number of parking space: 349
  • Infrastructure: 2315 м2
  • Apartaments: 45376 м2
  • Sector: 4
The futuristic architecture of the mirror building Imperia Tower has become one of the masterpieces of the world famous American architectural bureau NBBJ. The architects had an interesting and ambitious goal: to develop the concept and design of the building, which will be unlike any in the world. And they managed to achieve an exceptional harmony between the elegant elliptical profile of the tower and elements of progressive high-tech, reflecting the minimalism and dynamism of the style.
The facades of buildings made of translucent hinged structures - aluminum profiles with a double glass pane, with a mild selective coating filled with argon. Advantages of such a coating - protection from ultraviolet radiation, effective heat and noise insulation.
Functional solutions have touched the internal space of buildings. It is based on the principle of free planning, which allows the maximum use of the premises of the complex. The maximum efficiency of the use of the premises is also facilitated by the rectangular shape of the floors with an internal column pitch of 7.2 to 8.1 m. The floor load is 530 kg / m2.
"Empire II" is designed in the form of two buildings with various functions on the common stylobate: in building A, oriented to the embankment, the hotel will accommodate 283 rooms and 242 apartments, and in building B - offices. The building is designed in the form of a trapezoid with an internal open courtyard.
