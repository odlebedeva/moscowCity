Neva Towers

  • Height: 338 m (Tower 1) и 290 m (Tower 2)
  • Years of construction: 2016-2020
  • Developer: Renaissance Development
  • Total area: 357 000 m2
  • Lift speed: 8 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 77 (Tower 1) и 63 (Tower 2)
  • Number of parking space: 2040 (in the underground part - 1700, in the ground - 340)
  • Infrastructure: 97 770 м2
  • Sector: 17-18
The configuration of the towers is emphasized laconically: the shape of the hull-plates is supplemented by small shifts of the central parts, creating the likeness of the skeleton of the towers, which are clothed on two sides by narrowing tiers. The proportions of the tiers, which with each turn increase, were chosen in accordance with the classical "golden section". The pursuit of beauty complicated the task for architects, who instead of the standard even distribution of technical floors along the height of the building had to place them on the upper levels of the tiers in accordance with the "golden" breakdown.

Particular attention in the development of project plans is paid to maximum energy savings. The cooling center of the "Neva" tower uses a free cooling method, with a variable consumption of fumes and condensers, which saves considerable energy. This clearly demonstrates the innovative nature of all the internal equipment of the building.
