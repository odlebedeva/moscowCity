Afimoll center

  • Height: 50 m
  • Years of construction: 2004-2009
  • Developer: AFI Development
  • Total area: 283182 m2
  • Lift speed: 2 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 6 aboveground and 4 underground
  • Number of parking space: 2750
  • Sector: 6.7.8
"Afimoll City" shopping and entertainment center is a unique project realized within the framework of an ambitious, flagship, European project - the Moscow-City business center. Multifunctionality and innovation of the shopping center favorably distinguishes it among themselves. It's total area is 283,182 square meters. Of these the leased area is 107208 square meters. Visitors are waiting for more than 400 stores.

Atrium with impressive shapes and sizes is located between the skyscrapers. There is an unusual dancing fountain with lighting, which turns on every half hour, in the central part of Atrium. A powerful jet of 32 meters high "hits" to the roof. This makes the object interesting for walks in all weathers and is suitable for organizing meetings.
