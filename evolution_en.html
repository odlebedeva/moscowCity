Evolution Tower

  • Height: 255 м
  • Years of construction: 2011-2015
  • Developer: «Snegiri» and «Palace City»
  • Total area: 153891 м2
  • Lift speed: 7 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 53
  • Number of parking space: 1292
  • Infrastructure: 21000 m2
  • Office Rooms: 64000 m2
  • Sector: 2, 3
The spiral shape of the Evolution Tower resembles a DNA molecule: two snow-white bands of opposite facades smoothly twist and unite in a naked metal structure above the roof. Visually, the tower seems to "rotate" around its axis by 156 degrees (each of its 52 levels - by 3 degrees relative to the underlying one). The final composition "Crown of the Tower" is crowned with two asymmetric "arches" spanning 41m, visually combining a single snow-white "ribbon" with two opposite facades in the likeness of a mathematical symbol of infinity.
The unique facade of the Tower made of cold-glazed glazing ensures the lightness and dynamics of the form flowing against the forces of gravity. The double convex curvature of the facade of the tower is provided with the help of absolutely flat double-glazed windows made of reflecting glass by the technology of their "cold" bending. When assembling a modular panel, the double-glazed window is placed in the opening of the frame, which is in a horizontal position, and under its own weight is deformed, taking the shape of the frame without any thermal impact. Maximum deformation of one corner of the glass unit from the plane is not more than 50 mm. In the end, the facade looks like a single shell of glass, curved in a spiral.
A continuous band of bent glazing with a constant inclination in the corners of the tower (approximately 14 degrees to the vertical) realizes an amazing optical illusion, reflecting the surrounding panoramas of Moscow inverted at an angle of 90 degrees to the horizon, that is vertically, which is also unprecedented for world architecture.
