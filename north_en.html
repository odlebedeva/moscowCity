North Tower

  • Height: 108 m
  • Years of construction: 2005-2008
  • Developer: «North Tower»
  • Total area: 135000 m2
  • Lift speed: 7 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 26 ground and 2 underground
  • Number of parking space: 674
  • Sector: 19
The building is distinguished by an unusual architecture: the building is crowned by a towering tower, in which under the transparent dome are located office premises. The central atrium of the tower has a height of 80 meters, which is a European record. The only "green" building in "Moscow City".
