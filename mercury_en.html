Mercury City Tower

  • Height: 338.82 m
  • Years of construction: 2006-2013
  • Developer: LLC Liedel Investments Limited
  • Total area: 173960 m2
  • Lift speed: 7 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 75 overground и 5 underground
  • Number of parking space: 437
  • Infrastructure: 3834 m2
  • Apartaments: 22619 m2
  • Sector: 14
Mercury City is so delicately inscribed in the surrounding space that none of the surrounding towers closes the view. An unusual form of construction and panoramic glazing of copper-gold color, using tinted and energy-saving glass units allow this tower to stand out even against the background of the most unusual buildings of the city.
46 000 m2 - the area of the building's glazing. It's more than seven football fields. The cost of glass with oxide coating is about 4000 euros per m2. Each double-glazed window is worth at least 14 000 euros.
3 cm is the maximum oscillation of the tower. Such stability is attached to the reinforced concrete structure of two independent skeletons (many skyscrapers have one skeleton). "Mercury" is not affraid of earthquakes up to 9 points on the Richter scale (in the official expertise - up to 6 points).
2 million LEDs in the media facade located on the 67-68th floors (each of which is double). This is the highest media facade in Europe.
For lifting water upward skyscraper is used cascade system. Its work is provided by 3 pumping stations. At each technical level there is a water storage tank for 75 m3.
