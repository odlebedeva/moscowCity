Tower on the embankment

  • Height: 85 m (tower "А"), 127 m (tower "В") and 268 m (tower "С")
  • Years of construction: 2003-2007
  • Developer: ENKA
  • Total area: 254000 м2
  • Lift speed: 7 meter per second
  • Number of floors: 17 (tower "А"), 27 (tower "В") and 59 (tower "С")
  • Appartaments: no
  • Sector: 10
Almost all the buildings in Moscow City are made in the architectural style of deconstruction, and only the "Tower on the Embankment" complex is designed in high-tech style. The highlight of the complex is the technical floors in the tower "B" and the tower "C", which have no glazing outside the buildings. They create an optical illusion at first glance, it seems that they make buildings fragile, but in fact they, on the contrary, make buildings durable.
• Tower "A". It is represented by the smallest skyscraper on 17 floors (height 85 meters), construction of which began in 2003. The building opened its doors to renters in mid-October 2005.
• Tower "B". It is represented by a skyscraper with panoramic tinted windows and a height of 117 m (27 floors). This is an average large building from the complex, in which there are elite restaurants, entertainment centers and offices of famous companies.
• Tower "C". The building rises 268 meters above the ground and consists of 59 floors. In this case, the structure also has an underground part, which goes down to 5 floors.
