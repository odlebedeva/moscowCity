Eurasia Tower

  • Height: 308.9 m
  • Years of construction: 2005-2014
  • Developer: «MosCityGroup»
  • Total area: 193405 m2
  • Lift speed: 7 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 72
  • Number of parking space: 953
  • Infrastructure: 8322 m2
  • Office Rooms: 82817 m2
  • Apartaments: 26907 m2
  • Sector: 12
The business center "Eurasia" is a stylish, laconic modern building, with a fairly simple, but from that even more spectacular visual solution. The Eurasia Tower is the tallest building in Europe with a metal frame around the perimeter of the building. The facade of the skyscraper is made of green glass in combination with a stone and light-transmitting structures, which favorably emphasizes the elegance of the building, called some "The Steel Peak".
Eurasia" is the only steel tower in the "Moscow City", all the rest of the concrete.
