Oko Tower

  • Height: 249m (North tower), 354m (South tower) and 44m (parking)
  • Years of construction: 2011-2016
  • Developer: Capital Group
  • Total area: 249000 m2
  • Lift speed: 6 meters per second
  • Number of floors: 49 (Norrth tower), 85 (South tower), 14 (parking)
  • Number of parking space: 3740
  • Apartaments: 122507 m2
  • Sector: 16
The sculptural composition of two independent towers is united by a transparent six-story stylobate. The facade of this public part of the building is divided into triangular planes, forming a "crystalline volume". The beveled faces and relief grooves of the facades, tandem of concrete and glass create a visual effect of infinity and elegance - the complex seems to soar, rushes to the sky, admiring the ease of construction. Additional elegance, lightness and elegance of the silhouette to both towers provide relief grooves - "honeycomb" along the perimeter of the facades. A unique architectural solution for the arrangement of towers diagonally and the use of multifunctional clear glass in them. Transparent glass with a pale blue hue is framed with an anodised aluminum window in natural color.
