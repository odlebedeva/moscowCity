Bagration bridge

  • Main span: 147 m
  • Total length: 214 m
  • Width: 16 m
  • Years of construction: 1992-1997
The bridge was constructed of glass and concrete according to the project of the architect B. I.Tkhor. Designer Trausz Vladimir Ilich. Named after the Russian commander PI Bagration. It connects the Krasnopresnenskaya embankment from the embankment of Taras Shevchenko. Opened in September 1997 to celebrate the 850th anniversary of Moscow.
It consists of two levels. The lower level is a glazed gallery throughout. On the lower level are installed horizontal travolators for the convenience of walking at this level. The upper level is partly glazed, there is an open observation deck on it.
